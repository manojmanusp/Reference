Case Studies

  • For a Nationalized bank in Middle East, we help modernize their existing banking portal using SharePoint. The site is implemented with SharePoint Variations/TermStore to handle Multilingual web pages. The website was a complete makeover including branding and developing a Dynamic Data driven System. The users can modify anything starting from logo to add/modify the Products, News, Advertisements and Jobs.
  • For a leading Sports Club in USA, we have created a SharePoint based employee portal for managing their internal announcements, events, employee directory & discounts and internal opportunities. The portal provides the pleasant experience of visualizing all their scheduled events in one place with the concept of Calendar Overlay in SharePoint.
  • A leading middle-east nationalized bank also manages a public facing portal for local Golf sport events. The site was built using as a multilingual portal using SharePoint. The portal offers Live Score Board, Gallery, News, Sponsorship and Spectators Board and extras.
  • We have utilized SharePoint to develop a Document Management System for a Manufacturing Industry in Singapore. The portal handles the process of creating equipment's. Creation of equipment involves lot of documents which needs to be approved in different level. We have managed complex approval using SharePoint/Nintex Document Workflow process.
  • For a Public Health Department in Middle East, we have developed a SharePoint based Policy Management System. This Portal is used in every hospital in the country to create and publish the Policies to their employees. Each policy undergoes complex approval workflow that was managed using Nintex workflow. The documents are approved, published, signed and archived in the system.
  • For a leading Mobile Network provider in Middle East we have created a SharePoint based portal enabling the clients a Rich, Dynamic and Business Logical Content Management System to publish their New Tariff Plans for Prepaid/Postpaid network plans and other services offered by provider.
  • For a leading call center company in USA, we have developed a Call Center and Help Line Application for their vast and various Supply chain products and Assets. This Portal has Workflows and Ticket Raising Component to give a more collaborative approach between the Employees, Peers and Customers.
