Coastal Marine Fund

The Coastal Marine Fund (CMF) is a sekf-insurance program for commercial fishing vesseles. Formed in 1993 CMF is comitted to the responsible operation of commercial fishing vessels - working for the elimination of careless and reckless operations.

CMF member-owners create and maintain funds for loss reimbursement and share the risk of loss and damage to their vessels. Members also assist fellow member's vessels in distress.
